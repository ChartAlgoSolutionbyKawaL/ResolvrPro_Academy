Kawaljeet Singh Ladhar

DVP – Business Intelligence @ Kotak Mahindra Bank

I didn't start in tech. I started teaching it. In 2004, fresh out of college, I stood in front of a classroom at Apetech Computer Institute and taught SQL, C++, HTML, and Visual Basic to beginners. That's where I learned something most "AI educators" never will: how to explain hard things simply.

Over the next 20 years, I built a career across industries that most people only read about:

🏦 BANKING
HSBC → Kotak Mahindra Bank. From database analyst to DVP. Built BI platforms managing billions in retail assets. Led teams of 27 across 8 products.
🌍 GLOBAL OPERATIONS
VFS Global – Head of Automation. Managed end-to-end automation across 30+ countries. Data modelling, vendor management, SLA compliance.
💼 IT SERVICES
Tata Consultancy Services – Team Leader. Commercial card analytics, regulatory reporting for Visa & Mastercard across Asia.
🏆 RECOGNITION
Kotak Young Leaders Council – 40 under 40. Shadow to Board of Directors. ISO Certified (14K & 18K). Managing automation projects and teams.

I've automated 30+ reports, built dashboards for C-suite executives, managed data ecosystems across continents, and turned manual processes into scalable systems. I know what real-world skills look like — because I've used them every day for two decades.

Then I asked myself: "Why aren't we teaching this to 14-year-olds?"

ResolvrPro is the answer. Not theory. Not slides. The same frameworks, tools, and problem-solving approaches I use at a DVP level — adapted for young minds. Because if a 15-year-old learns this now, they won't spend 10 years catching up like I did.
